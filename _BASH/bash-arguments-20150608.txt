----------------------------------------------------------------
Arg		Meaning
----------------------------------------------------------------
$0		Represents command or scriptname
$1 to $9	Represents positional arguments from 01 to 09
${10}		Represents positional arguments from 10 onwards
$#		Represents the total number of arguments
$*		Represents all arguments
$$		Represents the PID of a running script
